Why SkillLink?

Unlike online courses (Skillshare, Udemy) or task-based services (TaskRabbit), SkillLink focuses on in-person interaction and mutual exchange.

Real communication and hands-on experience
Fair exchange without monetary transactions
Building a real community in your city
Flexibility: teach and learn simultaneously
